The Origin

Single-Origin Cacao Fields

Our cacao beans are sourced directly from sustainable, independent micro-lot farms in Madagascar, Ecuador, and Peru. Each terroir brings its own distinctive fingerprint of fruit, floral, or earthy notes that we work to preserve through meticulous fermentation and roasting processes.

  • Direct trade with 15+ micro-lot farmers
  • 100% traceable cacao supply chain
  • Sustainable farming practices

The Craft

Slow Handcrafted Tempering

Our truffles are hand-rolled and hand-dipped in small batches using centuries-old French techniques combined with modern flavor science. Each piece is tempered to exact specifications, creating the perfect snap and texture that melts at body temperature.

  • Small-batch handcrafting
  • Traditional French tempering methods
  • Fresh daily production
