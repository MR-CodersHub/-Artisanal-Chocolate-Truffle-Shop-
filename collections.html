Palate Architecture

The Cacao Tasting Flight

Experience our single-origin chocolate through our chef's curated sensory sequence.

1

Purify the Palate

Sip warm water or unsweetened green tea to cleanse the palate.

2

Inhale the Aromatics

Break the chocolate and inhale near the break line to detect fruity and woody notes.

3

Melting & Snap Analysis

Place a piece on the tongue and let it melt slowly, noting the texture shift from silk to cream.
