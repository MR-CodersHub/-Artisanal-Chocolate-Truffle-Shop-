Detailed Recipe Guide

The Velvet Cacao & Salted Caramel Tart

Crafted by Head Chocolatier Anelia Vance, this decadent tart balances crisp pastry with rich caramel and glossy single-origin ganache.

Step 1

The Cocoa Pâté Sablée

In a mixer, beat 120g softened butter, 75g icing sugar, and 25g ground almonds until creamy. Add 1 organic egg, mix until combined, then fold in 200g plain flour and 30g cocoa powder. Chill for 1 hour. Roll out to 3mm thickness, press into a tart ring, and bake blind at 170°C for 15-18 minutes. Let cool.

Step 2

The Citrus-Infused Caramel Layer

Caramelise 150g white sugar in a heavy saucepan until dark amber. Carefully whisk in 100g warm heavy cream, a pinch of Maldon salt, and the zest of half an orange. Let boil for 1 minute, then stir in 30g butter. Pour into the cooled tart shell and refrigerate for 30 minutes until set.

Step 3

The Glossy Ganache Glaze

Heat 120g double cream until simmering. Pour over 120g chopped Cacao Artisanal Madagascar 72% chocolate. Let stand for 2 minutes, then stir gently from the center outward until completely smooth. Stir in 15g softened butter for extra gloss. Pour over the caramel layer, spread evenly, and let set in the fridge for 2 hours.
